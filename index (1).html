The Challenge

Why SparkTrack Exists

Filipino households are in the dark — not just about their appliances, but about where their money goes every month.

💸
High Electricity Bills

The Philippines has one of the highest electricity rates in Southeast Asia at ~₱0.21/kWh. Bills arrive monthly but consumption happens daily — by the time you see the bill, it's too late.

🔍
Limited Visibility

Most Filipino households have no way to track per-appliance energy consumption. Without visibility, there is no control — making it almost impossible to reduce expenses or adopt energy-saving habits.

📱
No Tools for Low-Income Families

Existing solutions like smart meters or international apps don't support Philippine electricity providers or provincial rate structures. Low-to-middle income households are left behind.

🌏
Environmental Impact

Energy inefficiency at the household level has a compound effect on the environment. Greater visibility leads to greener choices — essential for sustainable development in the Philippines.

What We Offer

Everything You Need to
Take Control

Six powerful modules, all in one app — designed for everyday Filipino families.

📊

Energy Dashboard

Real-time daily, weekly, and monthly electricity usage visualized in clean, easy-to-read charts. Know exactly how much you're spending before the bill arrives.

🏠

Appliance Tracker

Log every appliance in your home and track its individual power consumption. Identify the biggest energy hogs in minutes.

🧮

Appliance Energy Calculator

Input usage hours and days to calculate the exact cost per hour, day, week, and month for any appliance — powered by PH provincial electricity rates.

📓

Personalized Saving Journal

Save your calculator results and monitor your savings progress over time. Your own personal energy finance diary.

🛒

Shop to Save

Get tailored appliance replacement recommendations with potential savings estimates, directly linked to Lazada, Shopee, and TikTok Shop.

💡

Fun Facts & Tips

Bite-sized energy-saving tips and fun electricity facts delivered through engaging notifications — making sustainability educational and fun.

Business Model

How SparkTrack Makes Money

Primary
Freemium Model
Free access to core features (energy dashboard, basic tracking). Premium subscription unlocks advanced analytics, unlimited appliance slots, personalized AI savings plans, and priority support.
Secondary
Affiliate Marketing
Commission-based revenue when users purchase energy-efficient appliances via affiliate links to Lazada, Shopee, and TikTok Shop. Aligns revenue with user savings outcomes.
